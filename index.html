About Us

Welcome to Northern Mystery – a premier dressage club inspired by the essence of the northern lights and the ancient wisdom of astrology. Guided by the glow of the cosmos, we blend grace, precision, and unity in both our growing dressage knowledge and competitive routines.

Our black, grey, white, and teal theme symbolizes clarity, chilled, and celestial energy — a brilliant reflection of the bond we share as riders. NM is led by Siri ‘Kappa’ Easter and co-owned by Lara ‘Tsunami’ Pearlpaw — our club is built on friendship, creativity, and a shared love for the northern lights.

Northern Mystery was branded on 25/05/25, a palindromic date that symbolizes symmetry and balance — qualities often tied to harmony, reflection, and spiritual alignment. At the very heart of Cupcake Valley, UK, is where starlit dreams and dressage elegance meet.

You’ll often find us parading through the arenas of Jorvik, forever expanding our knowledge and passion for Star Stable Dressage (SSD). We welcome every rider, no matter your dressage experience — whether we are your very first club or the final one you choose to call home.

Whether you're a seasoned competitor or a stargazing soul drawn to the rhythm of the arena, there's a place for you in our constellation. For those drawn to the stars — your journey begins here. Step into the light, and find your place among the Eternal Auroras! “We Don’t Follow the Aurora. We Are the Aurora.”
